## High-Level Findings

* Earnings beats result in positive returns on average, but gains are modest.
* Misses tend to lead to negative reactions.
* Overall win rate (~54%) is only slightly better than random.

## Company-Level Patterns

* **Tesla:** Highest average return but high volatility.
* **Microsoft:** Most consistent performer.
* **Google:** Underperforms despite frequent beats.

## Market Behavior

* "Sell the news" occurs in a significant share of earnings beats.
* EPS surprise explains only a small portion of price movement.
* Timing earnings trades is unreliable compared to company selection.

## Takeaway

Earnings announcements create volatility, not predictable profit opportunities.
